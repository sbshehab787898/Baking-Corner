The Atelier Charter

Engagement with Baking Corner Luxury Atelier constitutes an acceptance of our pursuit of perfection. These terms govern the standards of our collaborative creation.

Bespoke Commissions

Each creation is a singular masterpiece. Due to the high level of artistry and the rare nature of our ingredients, all custom commissions require a minimum advance notice of 48 hours. Commissions are non-refundable once the production cycle has commenced.

Quality Guarantee

We guarantee the absolute integrity of every item that leaves our atelier. However, confections are delicate sculptures; we are not responsible for structural changes due to improper climate control or transit once the item has been accepted at the delivery point.

Intellectual Property

All designs, recipe signatures, and aesthetic photography presented by Baking Corner are the intellectual property of the atelier. Reproductions for commercial use without written liaison are prohibited.
